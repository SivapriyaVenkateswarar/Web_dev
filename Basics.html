Are you lost?

I am intertwined between the frail strings of roguery and morality, contemplating what is right for the universe and wrong for humanity. My home is a cavern carved from splintered cathedrals. Locals and strangers; I decide on the ones that stay and the bunch to leave. "Who are you?", an inane human asks, "A being that your interned sense cannot understand.", I reply, "Why are you here?", it utters another unavailing question, "To recite the anecdotes of the abyss.", I answer with a composure built from comprehending generations of the unknown and known.

"You are a poet."
"Yes, I am."
